WHAT'S YOUR TYPE?

When you sign up, you will be asked to take a quick quiz that will type out your personality. This will provide you with valuable information about your personality and motivations ( why you do what you do). So you can better understand why you interact with others the way you do.

HARMONIZE

Play with our Harmonizer tool to test your compatibility with the people in your life. We all need different things. For example, during a fight: I may want to be left alone, when all you want is a hug.. Our Harmonizer will point out those kinds of characteristics so you will know how to best communication with them whether to inspire them, or resolve a conflict.

Harmony in the home or workplace is achievable when you understand what different people need and the best way interact with them. Perhaps your mother needs alone time when she's stressed, but you just want a hug. Self awareness and understanding people's actions allows for a new level of communication. We want you to incredibly deep and powerful relationships with everyone that is important to you.

LIFE GRAPH

You can customize your feed to filter updates from the people you care about the most. Each tier of the life graph indicates how close you are to each person and how frequently they will show up in your feed, so you aren’t constantly have to read your uncle in law’s political rants.
